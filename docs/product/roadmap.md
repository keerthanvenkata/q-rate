# Product Roadmap & Timelines

## Version 0 (Month 1-2): The Hook

- **Features:** Staff PWA (Keypad), Gemini Screenshot Verifier, Basic PostgreSQL Schema.
- **Focus:** Scaling 5-star Google reviews in 3 pilot Hyderabad cafes.
- **Goal:** Prove ROI to owners.
- **Key Feature:** "Magic Link Generation" - Direct-to-review URL to minimize clicks.

## Version 1 (Month 3-5): The Loop

- **Features:** Multi-visit Stamp Cards, Tiering (Silver/Gold/Roast Master), Admin Dashboard for Owners.
- **Focus:** "Customer Stickiness." Move from review-focus to visit-focus.
- **Logic:** Smart Remindersâ€”if a customer hasn't visited in 14 days, send a "We miss you" nudge.

## Version 2 (Month 6+): The Platform

- **Features:** Native Wallet Integration, `qrate.in/explore` discovery portal, Cross-cafe rewards.
- **Focus:** Lead generation. Sending users from one Q-Rate cafe to another.
- **Cross-Pollination:** "You liked Coffee at _Cafe A_? Try the desserts at _Cafe B_."
